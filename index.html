About Me

When I embroider, I feel a deep sense of calm and focus. Each stitch becomes a part of my soul, and the colors bring to life the emotions I want to convey. Embroidery gives me a sense of creative flow, allowing me to forget about everyday worries. Always, while working, I feel joy and inspiration as I see my ideas come to life. Each new piece brings me satisfaction and pride in the result, and the process of embroidery becomes a way to express my feelings and experiences.

Heart-knit. Hand-made. For you.

Knitting is my peace in the chaos of the world. I knit from the heart, putting care and warmth into every stitch. Each piece is not just an item, but a reflection of my feelings and mood. The threads in my hands come alive in colorful patterns that tell stories of love, tenderness, and care. Knitting gives me a sense of flow, when thoughts quiet down and the heart speaks. It’s a time when the world around slows down, and there is only me, the yarn, and the movement that creates warmth. My goal is to create beauty not only for myself but also to help those in need. That’s why I knit a sweater in my favorite color for myself and at the same time gift the same sweater to a child for free. Every piece I make is a part of me, my language of love that warms and inspires.
